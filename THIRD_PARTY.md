## TinyBVH builder controls

The optional production-builder bridge was developed against:

- Project: [jbikker/tinybvh][tinybvh]
- Revision: `0e4584287823252cf83f0e9cd072848bec5f79c5`
- Version: 1.8.0
- License: MIT
- Copyright: Copyright (c) 2024-2026, Jacco Bikker / Breda University of
  Applied Sciences

The header is an external build dependency and is not included in the curated
RayMMA source release. RayMMA's bridge invokes TinyBVH's binned-SAH and
spatial-split builders, then converts the result to RayMMA's uncompressed BVH8
layout. It does not use TinyBVH's production GPU traversal implementation.
Retain TinyBVH's complete MIT notice when redistributing its source or a
derived distribution.

## Other external dependencies

CMake, Python, Blender and its `bpy` module, the CUDA Toolkit, and the NVIDIA
driver are separately distributed tools or libraries. The optional viewers
also use system-provided OpenGL, GLFW, libjpeg, and libpng. These dependencies
are not vendored and retain their own licenses and distribution terms. Review
their notices before distributing binaries.

The default headless benchmark does not require Blender, TinyBVH, OpenGL,
GLFW, libjpeg, or libpng.
