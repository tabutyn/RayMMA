## TinyBVH production builders

The optional production-builder bridge was developed against:

- Project: [jbikker/tinybvh][tinybvh]
- Revision: `0e4584287823252cf83f0e9cd072848bec5f79c5`
- Version: 1.8.0
- License: MIT
- Copyright: Copyright (c) 2024-2026, Jacco Bikker / Breda University of
  Applied Sciences

The header is an external build dependency and is not included in the curated
RayMMA source release. RayMMA's bridge invokes TinyBVH's binned-SAH and
spatial-split builders and converts the result to the common benchmark layout.
Retain TinyBVH's complete MIT notice when redistributing its source or a
derived distribution.

## Benchmark scenes

No benchmark model or texture is covered by the RayMMA MIT License.

The procedural `Grid` scene is generated by RayMMA and is the only scene
enabled in the default build. Optional scene files must be obtained separately:

| Scene | Source and credit | Reported license | Included? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Crytek Sponza | Frank Meinl/Crytek; Morgan McGuire Computer Graphics Archive | CC BY 3.0 | No |
| Sibenik Cathedral | Marko Dabrovic; corrections by Kenzie Lamar and Morgan McGuire | CC BY-NC | No |
| San Miguel 2.0 | Guillermo M. Leal Llaguno; later archive contributors | CC BY 3.0 | No |
| Checker L/M/H | User-supplied GLBs with embedded textures | Rights must be established by the user | No |

Sibenik's noncommercial restriction is one reason it is not a required or
redistributed input. The Computer Graphics Archive requests citation as:

> Morgan McGuire, Computer Graphics Archive, July 2017,
> https://casual-effects.com/data

Review the archive's current per-scene terms before downloading or using any
scene. Results should record the exact file hash as well as attribution.

## Other external dependencies

CMake, Python, Blender, the CUDA Toolkit, and the NVIDIA driver are separately
distributed tools or libraries. The default headless benchmark does not
require Blender or TinyBVH.
